Personalized Portrait Generator: Realistic Speech-to-Portrait Generation via Face Prior Guided Diffusion Model



Abstract

Our work introduces SCFP, a Speech-Conditioned Facial Portrait Generation model, tackling the complex task of creating personalized portraits matching given speech inputs. To allevate the challenge of output diversity of LDM, SCFP incorporates a tailored Face Prior to guide the generation and employs a sample-specific weighting strategy to enhance speaker discriminability by capturing subtel facial variations contained in speech. And a ConRe pre-training strategy is adopted to jointly learn aligned speech-face representations and refine detailed facial features.

Note

  • A novel speech-condition LDM, namely Speech-Conditioned portrait generation with Face Prior guidance (SCFP), to formulate the LDM as a personalized portrait generator.
  • A sample-adaptive weighted module is designed to dynamically weighted the face prior, emphasizing the subtle personalized variance conditioned in speech for further identity preservation.
  • A ConRe pre-training procedure that aims to align speech and face representations while simultaneously reconstructing facial structures.
